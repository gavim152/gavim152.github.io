puzzle type is an innovative web font inspired by the powerlessness and impotence that comes when designing fonts for the web, as supposed to designing for print. this typeface seeks to explore this difference, creating a font by altering, skewing, breaking and shifting a traditional sans-serif.

the process of this type consisted of combining the familiar elements of classic sans-serif typeface-very popular in both web and print-and re-inventing them by splitting them in half. The disortotion process occurs by slicing a familar letter form in half and rearraging the vertical and horizontal spaces.

The words used to display this typeface are appropiately: inspired by the web. I have gathered the most used words in the web from the last 10-15 years as means to display this web-inspired font. It is very interesting to see the increidble breadth and scope these group of words have- from comical to critical.

to play the game you must choose one of the internet words you want. This will take you to a puzzle which you need to solve- the puzzle word will be related to the internet word you chose. You will find a couple of clues and some other hidden ones.. Once you think you got it, you click on the solve button!
