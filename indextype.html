NYU Tisch School of the Arts presents the Visual Language of The French New Wave Conference. Special Guestes Kai Bernau, Eli Roth and Wes Anderson will host one lecture each focusing in one element of this cinematic movement. After each lecture there will be 30 minutes of Q&A. The conference will be held at the Tishman Auditurium at 40 Washington Square S, New York, NY 10012. The Conference will start at 12pm and it will last aproximetely until 7pm.
